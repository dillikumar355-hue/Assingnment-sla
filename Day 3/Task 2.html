Mohsin Naqvi apologises to BCCI but adamant on not returning Asia Cup trophy

October 01,2025 | By The India Today

Pakistan Cricket Board chief Mohsin Naqvi has apologised to the Indian cricket board for what transpired after the Asia Cup Final on Sunday, September 28. India Today has learnt that Naqvi took a softer stance in the ACC meeting after ugly scenes in the Asia Cup Final, where he did not allow India to celebrate their victory over Pakistan with the winner's trophy.
