Steps to cook chicken biriyani

  1. First wash and clean the chicken through water thoroughly
  2. Marinate the chicken with curd, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, salt, and biryani masala. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Wash and soak basmati rice for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil or ghee in a pan, fry sliced onions until golden brown, and set aside for garnishing.
  5. In the same pan, sauté whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom) and add the marinated chicken.
  6. Cook the chicken until its half done.
  7. In a separate pot, boil water with salt and cook the soaked rice till its 70% done. Drain the water.
  8. Layer the chicken and rice in a large pot—start with chicken, then rice, and sprinkle fried onions, mint, and coriander leaves.
  9. Sprinkle saffron milk or food color over the top for flavor and color.
  10. Cover tightly and cook on low flame (“dum”) for 20-25 minutes.
  11. Gently mix and serve hot with onion pachadi.
